Jobzzy Global Business Agreement

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Non-Refundable Commitment

All payments made for "Brand Verification" (Blue Tick) and "Job Boosting" are final. Since Jobzzy provides instant platform authority and digital visibility, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances, including business closure or accidental purchase.

2. Professional Conduct & Anti-Harassment

Brands agree to interact with Job Seekers with the highest level of respect. Any report of rudeness, verbal abuse, or unprofessional behavior toward applicants will lead to an immediate, permanent ban. Jobzzy maintains a "Zero-Tolerance" policy for workplace bullying.

3. Global Labor Compliance

By using Jobzzy, you certify that your business abides by the International Labour Organization (ILO) standards. You shall not solicit work from minors, and you must offer fair wages as per your local jurisdiction's laws. You are prohibited from asking for "application fees" from seekers.

4. Data Privacy & Security

You are granted access to seeker resumes for hiring purposes only. Sharing, selling, or leaking applicant contact information is a violation of the Global Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Violators will be prosecuted to the full extent of the law.

5. Termination

Jobzzy reserves the right to strip the "Blue Tick" verification from any brand that accumulates more than 3 verified complaints from users regarding the hiring process.
